Mutton Momos

Cooking Method

Mutton Momos is a dumpling stuffed with minced mutton. Momo is a South Asian dumpling popular among the Indian Sub Continent. Momo is stuffed with various fillings of oneâ€™s choice, steamed and served with momo sauce.

Ingredients

  • Mutton - 250g (Boiled and Minced)
  • Onion - 1/2 cup (chopped)
  • Carrot - 1/2 cup (chopped)
  • Cabbage - 1/2 cup (chopped)
  • Coriander Leaves - 1/4 cup (chopped)
  • Lemon Juice - 1/2 cup
  • Pepper Powder - 1 tsp
  • Soy Sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Salt
  • Dumpling Dough:-
  • Salt
  • Water - Enough to Knead the dough

Directions

    Step 1
  1. Mix flour, salt and water, knead to a medium consistency dough. The dough should neither be too soft nor too hard. Cover and keep aside for 15 minutes.

    2. Step 2
  2. To prepare the stuffing, take the minced mutton to a bowl, add onion, other veggies and mix well.

    3. Step 3
  3. Add salt, lemon juice, pepper powder, soy sauce, salt and mix well. Keep aside.

    4. Step 4
  4. Dust some flour on a flat surface and roll the dough to a medium level crepe, not too thick nor too thin. Cut a medium size discs from the the crepe.

    5. Step 5
  5. Stuff a spoon of mutton mix to a disc and seal the edges accordingly. Continue the process until done.

    6. Step 6
  6. Steam all the momos in different batches for about 20 minutes. Serve hot with momos chutney. Enjoy!
