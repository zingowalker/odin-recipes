Chicken Biryani

Cooking Method

Chicken biryani is a dish of mixed rice, meat and vegetables cooked in a thick gravy. It is a common Indian dish. It is a very popular dish in the Indian subcontinent and the world.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 medium tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1 (2 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken pieces cut into chunks
  • 2 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 pinch powdered saffron
  • 5 pods cardamom
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 1 (1 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 kg basmati rice
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt

Directions

    Step - 1
  1. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the onions and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 5 minutes. Add the ginger, cinnamon stick, and cinnamon stick and cook, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the ground black pepper, chili powder, turmeric, cumin, and salt and cook, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the tomatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes are soft and fragrant, about 5 minutes. Add the yogurt, cardamom, and mint and cook, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the chicken pieces and cook, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.

    2. Step - 2
  2. Add the vegetable oil to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until the oil is hot and fragrant, about 2 minutes. Add the onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the saffron and cook, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the cardamom, cloves, cinnamon stick, and ginger and cook, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the rice and cook, stirring occasionally, until the rice is cooked through, about 5 minutes. Add the chicken stock, salt, and 1 teaspoon of the chicken stock to the rice and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 20 minutes.

    3. Step - 3
  3. Serve the rice with the chicken pieces and garnish with the mint. Enjoy!
